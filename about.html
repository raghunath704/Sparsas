Puja Das - Founder & Artist

The Heart of Handmade

In a world of mass production, we believe in the magic of handmade. Each piece we create tells a story, imbued with the passion, dedication, and unique touch of the artisan. Handmade items carry a soul, a connection between the creator and the recipient, making them truly special and cherished. They represent a commitment to quality, craftsmanship, and the preservation of traditional skills.

Our Story

Sparsa's was born from a deep love for creating beautiful and meaningful objects. Our founder, Puja Das, has always been drawn to the tactile nature of crafting, finding joy in transforming raw materials into works of art. What started as a personal passion soon blossomed into a desire to share her creations with the world.
